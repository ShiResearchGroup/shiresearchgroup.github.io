Welcome to the DP Lab (Digital Porous Materials Lab) @ University at Buffalo! We are a computational and theoretical research group. We leverage machine learning, quantum and molecular-level modeling, statistical mechanics, and mathematical modeling:

  • To gain fundamental understanding at the interface of materials science and chemical physics.
  • To develop cutting-edge porous materials solutions to major societal problems facing our planet.
    We are also an affiliate member of the Acceleration Consortium, an initiative to accelerate the discovery of advanced materials and molecules with the power of AI, robotics, and advanced computing.
